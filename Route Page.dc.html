Practical basics

Good to know

Taxi

Bolt (main) + Yandex Go. Download before you fly.

Money

Georgian lari (GEL); cards everywhere, cash for markets & taxis. (Yerevan: Armenian dram)

Visa

EU: none. Other passports: check.

Insurance

Required from Jan 2026: health + accident insurance to enter Georgia — min 30,000 GEL cover, valid your whole stay. Sort it before you fly; no cover, no boarding.

SIM

Magti / Geocell at the airport.

Weather

Tbilisi +12–20°C; Kazbegi 0–10°C, maybe snow. Warm layers + swimsuit for the baths.
